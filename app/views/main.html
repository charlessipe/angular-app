Survey Pop allows you to easily collect feedback from your customers.

  • 1. Give your customers a card with the survey code.
  • 2. Customers go to the URL on the card to fill out the survey.
  • 3. A gift card is automatically mailed to the customer.
  • 4. Review your customer's feedback in the user dashboard.
